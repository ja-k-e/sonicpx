sonic pixels

sonic pixels translates audio into images and images into audio. audio samples are stored in image pixels, which can then be played back as audio.

audio to image

record an audio message as an image. stop whenever or max out at 60 seconds.

convert wav or mp3 file into an image. choose then hit play button.

▶

image to audio

play an image as audio. load an image then play to hear what the image sounds like. to play it again, you have to re-add the file. be warned, random images will not sound good and might get a little loud.

▶

right click generated images to save to your computer. as long as the image remains exactly as it was saved, it should be playable in full quality at.
